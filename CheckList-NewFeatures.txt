1) “For You” unified personalized feed****

New page: For You tab that:
Uses saved topics + sources from personalization.
Calls your /api/news endpoint with those preferences.
Shows a mix of:
Top headlines in chosen topics.
A small “Recommended for you” section (maybe call OpenRouter to re‑rank).
Components to add:
ForYouFeed component that accepts favoriteTopics and composes the feed.
“Fine‑tune” button linking back to Personalization. 

2) “My Activity” / history****

New tab under settings or notes:
Shows recent articles you opened (persist minimal metadata to Supabase, or local storage).
Shows your recent AI summaries or “Most summarized topics”.
Value: makes the app feel like your personal news workspace, not just a feed. 

3) Saved searches / alerts (simple version)**

On search page:
“Save this search” button → store query + filters to Supabase.
Section listing “Saved searches” with quick chips/tags to rerun.
(Optional later) Email or in-app “badge” when there are new articles matching a saved search. 

4) Better note integration in article cards****

On each article card, show:
A badge/indicator if you already have a note on that article.
“Quick note” popover to create/edit a note without leaving the feed. 

5) Plans / monetization prep (even if you stay free)**

You already have a plans page. Before deployment:
Clarify tiers/features (e.g. Free vs Pro, AI limits, extra personalization).
